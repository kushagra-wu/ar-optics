How to use
  1. Open in Chrome on Android (with ARCore) or a WebXR-capable headset browser.
  2. Tap Enter AR, then tap a wall to place the screen.
  3. Tap again at any point in front of the wall to place the lens. Focal length is set automatically (F = ½ wall-to-lens distance).
  4. The phone is the object — walk closer/farther to see the image change. Toggle Convex / Concave at any time.
  5. Tap Snap to freeze the scene; the candle and 3 principal rays appear so you can walk around the diagram. Tap Resume Aim to keep moving.
